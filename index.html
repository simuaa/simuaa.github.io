Numerical simulations in Astrophysics in France

This is the webpage of the french astrophysical community involved in numerical simulations. It provides news (workshops, schools, links, reports...) of interest for researchers working in France and involved in high performance computing in astrophysics. People can register to the "simuaa" mailing list (here)
