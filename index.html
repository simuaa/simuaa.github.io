Numerical simulations in Astrophysics in France

This is the webpage of the french astrophysical community involved in numerical simulations. It provides news (workshops, schools, links, reports...) of interest for researchers working in France and involved in high performance computing in astrophysics. People can register to the "simuaa" mailing list (here).

This webpage is an initiative of the INSU-AA working group "HPC in astrophysics" composed of G. Aulanier, J. Blaizot, B. Dintrans, G. Dubus, S. Fromang, P. Hennebelle, F. Le Petit, A. Marchaudon, J.-P. Vilotte. As it is still largely in construction, any suggestions and/or contributions are welcome. See this page to contact us.


Latest news:

  • Numerical simulations workshop, organized during the french astronomy week, to be held in Lyon in June, will host a workshop devoted to numerical simulations in astrophysics on June 16th and 17th 2016. The full program is now available online (deadline: 15 May, 2016)

This webpage is still in construction. We are planning to add a page that will display results obtained by french teams by means of numerical simulations. If you wish to contribute, please send us an image along with a few lines of description, credits, and a link to a publication.
